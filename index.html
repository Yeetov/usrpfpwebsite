Our Mission

Team Gelatofish is a team of passionate Discord users. We believe customization shouldn't be locked behind a paywall, so we foster community creativity by building tools that allow users to express themselves freely.

UserPFP

UserPFP is a client modification that unlocks animated profile pictures on Discord without Nitro. It also supports API images and GIFs (such as random duck or cat images), visible to all other users of the mod.
